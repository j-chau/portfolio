About Me

My name is Joey Chau and I am a web developer.

Now, armed with finely tuned skills in technical design and analytical problem-solving, I am ready to take on the web and continue to create beautiful, universally accessible websites.

When I am not coding, you can find me somewhere along the Niagara Escarpment, climbing rocks, petting dogs, and photographing mushrooms.
